History of TechCon

TechCon began as a small gathering of innovators and entrepreneurs in the early 2000s. What started as a local meetup quickly grew into a recognized platform for exploring the latest advancements in technology. Over the years, TechCon has expanded from general discussions about emerging tech to specialized tracks covering Artificial Intelligence, Digital Health, Semiconductors, and Deep Tech.
